How to Help

To help us you don't have to donate money, just make your commute greener by following our suggestions.

Take a Walk

Save money and burn extra calories walking to work. You are not only helping the Environment but also your well-being.

Learn More

Go Biking

If your office is not within walking distance, biking is just as helpful and have the same positive effect on you and the Environment.

Learn More

Public Transportation

Public transportation reduces the number of cars in street helping improve air quality, alleviate traffic congestion, and noise.

Learn More

Carpooling

Carpooling not only saves money and reduces congestion but also gives you the opportunity to develop new friendships with co-workers or other commuters.

Learn More

Telecommuting

For each person who works from home there is an office space that does not need to be constructed, heated, or cooled.

Learn More

Move Closer to Work

Not only you spend less time commuting but can also commute by bike or walking.
